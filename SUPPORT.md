# ZENTRA support

Looking for help on usage, capabilities or even configuration of your ZENTRA instance?

You can ask on:
- [ZENTRA forums](https://forum.zentra-project.org),
- [ZENTRA Telegram channel](https://t.me/zentraen),
- [ZENTRA IRC channel](http://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=zentra) #zentra on Freenode,
- Subscribe to one of [ZENTRA mailing lists](https://zentra-project.org/community).

You may also be looking for [ZENTRA documentation](https://zentra-project.org/resources/#documentation).
